## Literature Search Tips & Tools

### Online Databases
- **Google Scholar** (https://scholar.google.com): Free, broad coverage
- **Web of Science** (institutional access): Citation tracking, impact
- **Scopus** (institutional access): Similar to Web of Science
- **PubMed** (biomedical, not needed here)
- **JSTOR** (institutional access): Historical coverage
- **arXiv** (https://arxiv.org): Preprints, physics/materials

### Search Strategies
1. **Keywords**: "high-entropy oxide" AND "thermoelectric" AND "transport"
2. **Author tracking**: Follow key authors (Yeh, Rost, Leibler, etc.)
3. **Reverse citations**: Google Scholar "Cited by" function shows impact
4. **Similar articles**: arXiv "related" feature, Google Scholar "Similar articles"

### Citation Management
- **Zotero** (free, open-source): Browser extension for one-click capture
- **Mendeley** (free tier): Reference organization, tagging, annotation
- **EndNote** (commercial): Legacy system, widely used in institutions
